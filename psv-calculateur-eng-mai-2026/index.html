Methodology

How these numbers are calculated

Salary costs

Gross salary prorated over the period, plus an estimate of benefits and related costs.

Remplacement

Recruiting time, interviews, onboarding, training, and additional management during the adjustment period.

Lost sales

Gap between expected quota and actual performance, converted into net profit using the company margin.

Indicative estimates only. Actual results vary by industry, sales cycle, territory, and level of internal support.
